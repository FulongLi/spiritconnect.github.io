        <h3>What is the Power Electronics AI Agent?</h3>
        <p>It is an intelligent design partner that assists engineers across the full power electronics
        stack — from device characterization to converter optimization to system-level architecture —
        using physics-guided AI models that learn and improve with each design cycle.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <h3>Do you sign NDAs?</h3>
        <p>Yes. We routinely work under NDA and respect export controls. Your proprietary information,
        designs, and datasets remain confidential.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <h3>Where does the training data come from?</h3>
        <p>Our models combine device datasheets, published research, public datasets, and private
        measurements. All models are physics-guided and validated against experimental hardware data.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <h3>On-prem vs cloud?</h3>
        <p>Both deployment options are available. Private datasets remain your intellectual property,
        and the AI Agent can run on your preferred infrastructure.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <h3>What accuracy can I expect?</h3>
        <p>Efficiency predictions typically match experimental results within 1–2% for well-characterized
        topologies. The agent continuously improves as more validation data feeds back into its models.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <h3>Can it integrate with our existing tools?</h3>
        <p>Yes. We provide APIs and CLI tools that connect the AI Agent to Python, MATLAB/Simulink,
        SPICE simulators, and custom in-house toolchains.</p>
